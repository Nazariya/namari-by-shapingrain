WHAT WE DO: OUR VISION

Nazariya aspires to bring out the subtle message behind these artforms; the message of unity, brotherhood and community.

In this world of digital art and 3D printing, Nazariya is a movement to bring us closer to the beautiful world of Indian Folk Art. It is an effort towards not just reviving the dying and extinct Indian artforms, but also towards making them relevant in the contemporary world. Nazariya aspires to bring out the subtle message behind these artforms; the message of unity, brotherhood and community. This is Nazariya's way of taking you on an incredible journey into India's past, helping us create a new future.

Our Mission

To celebrate the rich heritage and indigenous cultures of India, one art form at a time.
